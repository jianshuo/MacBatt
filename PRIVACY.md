# Privacy Policy for MacBatt

**Last updated:** April 5, 2026

## Overview

MacBatt is a macOS menu bar application that displays battery and power information for your MacBook. Your privacy is important to us — MacBatt is designed to work entirely on your device with no data collection whatsoever.

## Data Collection

MacBatt does **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any data. Specifically:

- **No personal data** is collected.
- **No battery or device data** leaves your Mac.
- **No analytics or tracking** of any kind.
- **No network connections** are made by the app.
- **No cookies or identifiers** are used.
- **No third-party SDKs** are included.

## How the App Works

MacBatt reads battery information directly from your Mac's hardware (via macOS IOKit) and displays it locally in the menu bar. All data is processed in memory and is never written to disk or sent anywhere.

## Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data.

## Children's Privacy

MacBatt does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the updated version will be posted at this URL. Since MacBatt collects no data, meaningful changes are unlikely.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue at [github.com/jianshuo/MacBatt](https://github.com/jianshuo/MacBatt/issues).
